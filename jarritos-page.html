Jarritos

Ad Campaign

Jarritos is a popular soft drink in the hispanic community, due to its tasty fruit flavored beverages, but is relatively unknown to other ethnic groups. This ad campaign focuses on the company’s unkown precesence and pairs each flavor, offered by Jarrito, with an unknown creature. These two aspects work together in order to get people intrigued with the drinks in hopes that it will convince them to try the drink. The campaign also uses a modified version of the company’s slogan.
