Piñata Dale

Identity System

Piñata Dale is a small piñata shack located in Mexico City, Mexico. They specialize in making custom order piñatas. The business was in need of an identity system that fits the wacky and playful vibe associated with piñatas. This identity system and contents within it are in spanish. Piñata Dale roughly translates to "Hit the piñata".
