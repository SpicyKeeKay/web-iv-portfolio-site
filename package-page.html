Salted Mountain

Package Design

Salted Mountain ,the producers and distributors of their own salt and pepper, was in need of containers that had a bold and masculine appearance. The target audience is men between the ages of 30-45 that enjoy activities like hunting, camping and hiking.

Concept

The concept for the labels was to create an imaginary lands that the salt and pepper came from. Customers will be intrigued and wonder if the salt was mined from the treacherous Goat’s Peak or the pepper harvested from the mysterious Black Forest.


The illustrations depict these lands as well as follow the same structure that the company’s logo has. They follow an upward pointed direction in order to capture a more imposing and dominant feeling. The paper used was chosen for its physical textures that would be felt when held, as well as how it complimented the corks color.
