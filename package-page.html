Salted Mountain

Package Design

Salted Mountain are the producers and distributors of their own salt and pepper. The company was in need of containers that had a bold and masculine appearance. The target audience is men between the ages of 30-45 that enjoy activities like hunting, camping and hiking.

The concept behind the labels was to create an imaginary land that the salt and pepper came from. Customers will be intrigued and wonder if the salt was mined from the treacherous Goat's Peak or the pepper harvested from the mysterious Black Forest. The illustrations on the labels depict these lands as well as follow the same basic structure that the company's logo has. Having them follow a upward pointed direction in order to capture a more imposing and dominant feeling. The paper used was chosen for the physical rugged textures that would be felt when held as well as how it complimented the corks color.
